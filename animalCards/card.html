Humpback Whale

Humpback whales migrate up to 25,000 km per year.

Humpback whales aren't the largest whale in the sea, but they are one of the most interesting. They can be found leaping out of the water, singing, or fishing using 'bubble nets' to trap fish in order to eat many in one gulp.
